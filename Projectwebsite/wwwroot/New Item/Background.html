The Chronicles of Etheria

In the ancient world of Etheria, where the skies shimmered with ethereal light and the land pulsed with arcane energy, five legendary beings shaped the destiny of all existence.

CHL, the Weaver of Shadows: Master of illusions and secrets, bending reality to his will.
WXY, the Stormcaller: Summons the fury of the heavens, commanding lightning and thunder.
WDS, the Architect of Time: Guardian of history, ensuring the balance between past and future.
YJ, the Flameborn Sage: Keeper of the eternal fire, forging weapons of unimaginable power.
LLX, the Celestial Wanderer: A traveler between dimensions, unlocking cosmic mysteries.

A great calamity looms over Etheria. Only by uniting their powers can they restore harmony, but as the prophecy foretells: “When the five awaken, the veil shall break… but will salvation come, or the end of all things?”
